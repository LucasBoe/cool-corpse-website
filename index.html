Values & The Triforce

The discourse about games has diversified. It is divided into numerous, homogeneous communities with their own mechanisms, requirements, and history. Individual areas of game culture may never have contact with each other. In others, solidified disputes about content, values & contexts are conducted. And in the middle of it all sit the people who create the games - us. In such a diverse field of work, it was therefore important for us to agree on a common ground on which to build.

For this purpose, we have developed the TRIFORCE, a matrix based on six elements, which on the one hand describes our vision, but on the other hand also serves as a fixed point for decisions. None of the elements is absolute, all are related to each other and will change over time. Nevertheless, the TRIFORCE unites our common ground as COOL CORPSE.

What we are working on...

Space Sprouts (AT)

Space Sprouts is a narratively loaded sandbox. A spaceship crammed with unexpected objects to experiment with. Be curious and discover the story behind your journey and your ship. But the space is limited and the flight won't last forever. What are you going to do?


We create a limited space that follows its own rules and regularities and give the players tools with which they can playfully explore but also change. It's about personal stories and self-efficacy but in space!


  • Production Start: 2023
  • Planned Release: 2024
